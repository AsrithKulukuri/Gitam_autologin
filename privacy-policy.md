Privacy Policy

GITAM AutoPass Pro does not collect, transmit, sell, or share user data with any third parties.

Any login-related information is stored locally on the user's device using Chrome storage and is used only to automate the login process on the official GITAM login portal.

No data is sent to external servers.
No analytics or tracking is performed.
No personal data is sold or shared.

If you have any questions, contact the developer via the GitHub repository.
